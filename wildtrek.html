Wild Treks

"Leave the road, take the trails. It's not the mountain we conquer, but ourselves."

Into the
Heart of the Wild

Our guided Wild tours take you into the heart of world-renowned wilderness destinations: Skardu, Deosai and the Cold Desert. Enjoy solitude and adventure as you embark on a self-sufficient journey deep into Pakistan's most stunning wild areas.

You will carry everything you need in a top-of-the-line fitted backpack we provide. Pack weights vary between 25–45 pounds depending on the area, duration, time of year, and your build. We provide everything except clothing, footwear, rain gear, and personal items — all other gear is top-of-the-line.

All meals are included from breakfast the first day through lunch the last day, and your expert guide will prepare every meal over the open fire.
